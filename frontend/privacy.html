B. COOKIE POLICY

Cookies and Web Beacons

The Website uses "cookie" and "web beacon" technology. Cookies are tiny text files that are stored on your device to help track your use of our site and to personalize your journey. Web beacons are used in combination with cookies to help website operators understand how visitors interact with their websites.

Our cookies serve various purposes, but are generally either necessary, help us improve performance, provide extra functionality, or help us serve relevant advertisements.

  • First Party Cookies: These are cookies controlled and managed by HQMX. We only use cookies that are strictly necessary for functionality.
  • Third Party Cookies: These are cookies controlled by a third party. Data collected is only accessible by the third party. We do not have access to or control over these cookies.

You can manage your cookie settings in your browser. However, refusing cookies may degrade the performance of our Services.
