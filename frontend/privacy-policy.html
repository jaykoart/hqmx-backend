B. COOKIE POLICY

Cookies and Web Beacons

The Website uses "cookie" and "web beacon" technology. Cookies are tiny text files that are stored on our website as you browse, to help track your use of our site and to personalize your journey. Web beacons are used in combination with cookies to help website operators understand how visitors interact with their websites.

We want to make sure you are fully aware of the cookies we use and what they do on HQMX. Our cookies serve various purposes, but are generally either necessary or essential to the functioning of our sites, services, applications, tools or messaging, help us improve the performance or provide you extra functionality of the same, or help us to serve relevant and targeted content.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • "Essential" cookies are essential for browsing on our website, especially for the functioning of media analysis and download processes.
  • "Analysis and Customization" cookies help you search more effectively and optimize your experience and help us better target your requirements.
  • "Functional" cookies are used to provide services or to remember settings to improve your visit, including maintaining your session across different domains.
  • "Performance" cookies gather statistical information about page visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site.
  • "Targeting" cookies may be set through our sites by third party partners to deliver content relevant to your specific interests.
